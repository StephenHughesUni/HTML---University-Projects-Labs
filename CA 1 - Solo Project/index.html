Corona Virus Level 5 Lockdown

Social and Family Gatherings

  • In your home:
    • No visitors to your home
    • Exceptions of visits for essential purposes
  • Other outdoor settings
    • Meet with 1 other household
  • No other social or family gatherings should take place.

Exercise and sporting events

  1. Training and matches.
    • Non-contact training can continue for school aged children, outdoors in pods of 15
    • Professional, elite sports - behind closed doors.
  2. All other training activites should be individual only.
  3. Gyms, leisure centers and swimming pools are closed.
